Hi, I'm Jenny.

I'm a junior at the University of Michigan & seeking summer 2020 internship opportunities. My double major is in Computer Science & English. Currently a software engineering intern at Mcity.


I enjoy solving problems using language.

Computer Science

I enjoy front end/full stack development, but I'm also interested in learning more about cyber security and machine learning. At Mcity, I build web applications using Vue. From past internships, I have experience with graphic design and UX/UI design. In addition, I am a member of Rewriting the Code, an alumna of Girls Who Code, and recently joined GEECS (Girls in Electrical Engineering & Computer Science) on campus.

Courses I've taken: Data Structures & Algorithms (EECS 281), Foundations of Computer Science (EECS 376), Programming & Intro Data Structures (EECS 280), Elementary Programming Concepts (EECS 183), Discrete Math (EECS 203), Calculus I & II

Courses I'm taking Fall 2019: Web Systems (EECS 485), Introduction to Computer Organization (EECS 370)

English & Writing

Learning to write well has helped me develop critical thinking skills that are useful to me as a software engineer, but I also like writing for writing's sake. My studies have focused on children's literature & the fantasy genre. During May 2017 to May 2019, I served as the Editor-in-Chief of the Gargoyle, U of M's official humor magazine. I have experience consulting with undergraduate and graduate students on writing projects of all genres.
